Buttons

Guidelines

Buttons play an important role in the user-journey and often represent the final step the user selecting a product or service. As such, there should be no confusion in the user's mind as to what tapping or clicking on that button will do.

Buttons may be:

  • Active primary
  • Active secondary
  • Inactive
