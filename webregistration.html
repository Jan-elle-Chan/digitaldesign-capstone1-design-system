Who Are We?

Caribbean Chef was launched in 2021 by Chef Mark in Vancouver. Chef Mark moved to Vacouver from the Caribbean when he was just 14 years old and has always loved cooking. Finding the right ingredients for Caribbean inspired food was always a challenge and as love for Carbbean food grew in Vancouver, he developed a line of products and cooking classes to fill that need.
