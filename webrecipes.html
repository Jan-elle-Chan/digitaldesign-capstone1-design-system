Calalloo

Quantity Ingredient
1 lg bundle dasheen bush
5 medium ochroes
3 medium pimentoes
1 cup coconut milk
3 cloves garlic
1 tbsp salt
1 large hot pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop dasheen bush leaves and stems, and ochroes
  2. Finely chop pimentoes and garlic
  3. Bring 2L of water to boil in a heavy saucepan
  4. Add dasheen bush, garlic and pimentoes to water
  5. Lower heat and allow to cook for 10 minutes
  6. Add salt, whole scotch bonnet and coconut milk
  7. Leave on low heat, mixing occasionally with a swizzle stick
  8. Remove from heat when cooked, usually 45 minutes

Macaroni Pie

Quantity Ingredient
450g macaroni
2 large eggs
2 Tbsp salt
1 cup milk
4 Tbsp butter
1 cup crushed crackers
5 cups grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Bring 3L of water to boil in a large pot
  2. Add salt and macaroni
  3. Lower heat and cook until tender
  4. Drain an place in large bowl
  5. Beat eggs and milk, add salt and black pepper
  6. Add milk mixture and cracker crumbs to macaroni
  7. Mix in 3 cups of cheese, pour into greased baking dish
  8. Top with cheese and bake for 30 mins at 350F
