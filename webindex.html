Who Are We?

Caribbean Chef was launched in 2021 by Chef Mark in Vancouver, British Columbia. Chef Mark moved to Vacouver from the Caribbean when he was just 14 years old and has always loved cooking. In his new country, he grew his passion for cooking, incorporating flavours from the Caribbean. However, finding the right ingredients was always a challenge.

What We Offer

As love for Caribbean food grew in Vancouver grew along with the Caribbean population there, he decided to develop a line of products and services to fill that need.

  • Products: utensils, cookbooks and ingredients
  • One Day, beginner and advanced cooking classes
  • Online recipes and videos
