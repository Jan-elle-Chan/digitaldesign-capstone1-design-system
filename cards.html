Cards

A card is an element used to display content of different types. Cards will be arranged on webpages using the flexbox model.

Cards may be styles in a variety of ways, and may be set as flex-containers themselves to manage the content in them. Preference is for:

  • Curved borders - border-radius:10px
  • If solid background colours or gradients are used, that they fall within the colour palette
