Carousels

A carousel of visual elements. These visual elements must be reflective of the Caribbean Chef brand.

The visual elements:

  • May occupy the entire width of the viewport on desktop version or homepage
  • May occupy the width of an entire card in the two-column or three-column layout.
  • Must not exceed 50% of the viewport height on a desktop
  • Follow style of carousel in code provided below
