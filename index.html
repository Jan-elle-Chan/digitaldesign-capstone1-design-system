Why do we need design systems?

Design systems provide developers with a single source of design information, which can be used to create and maintain digital products for an organisation.

They are used to:

  • Ensure qaulity and consistency
  • Train new team members
  • Reduce development time

Wave DS overview

The Wave DS was developed for Caribbean Chef Ltd as a set of design guidelines for the creation of their website.
